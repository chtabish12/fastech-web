      FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions):

      • Q: Are solar panels suitable for all types of buildings?
        A: Solar panels are suitable for a wide range of buildings, including residential homes, commercial buildings, and industrial facilities. Our team of experts will assess your specific requirements and recommend the best solar panel system for your building.
      • Q: How much can I save on my electricity bills by installing solar panels?
        A: The amount of savings depends on various factors such as the size of the solar panel system, your electricity consumption, and the amount of sunlight in your area. However, it's common for solar panel owners to significantly reduce their electricity bills and even generate excess energy that can be fed back into the grid.
      • Q: What is the warranty period for your solar products?
        A: We offer a 10-year product warranty and a 25-year performance warranty for our solar panels. Our inverters come with a 5-year warranty. Additionally, we provide a complimentary one-year Service Level Agreement (SLA) free of cost.
      • Q: Do you provide installation services?
        A: Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products. Our team of skilled technicians will ensure that your solar panel system is installed correctly and efficiently, maximizing its performance and longevity.
      • Q: Can solar panels work during cloudy days or in low light conditions?
        A: Solar panels can still generate electricity even in cloudy or low-light conditions. While the efficiency may be slightly reduced, solar panels can still harness the available sunlight to produce power. Additionally, excess energy generated on sunny days can be stored and used during periods of lower sunlight.
      • Q: What maintenance is required for solar panels?
        A: Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning to remove dirt and debris, along with periodic inspections, is usually sufficient to keep them operating at their best. Our team can guide the recommended maintenance practices to ensure optimal performance.

      If you have any more questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our knowledgeable team.
